EASY TO FIND

From the South:  Take I-90 to Osseo interchange.  Follow County 'R' north to Augusta.  There, take Hwy. 27 north to the village of Radisson.  Turn north on Co 'H' and follow to Co 'CC', turn right and continue to The Landing.

From the North:  From Hayward, take Hwy 'B' east for 15 miles to Hwy. 'CC', then follow 'CC' south for 5 miles to The Landing.
