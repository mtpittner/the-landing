Formerly Herman's Landing, the restaurant has wrap-around views of the Chippewa Flowage, with seating for up to 80 inside or lakeside seating outside for 80.  Drive, boat, or fly in...you'll like our prices and customer service.

Open daily at 7:00 am for breakfast, lunch, and fish, seven days a week, between May to October. We have a full menu and daily specials, and our "not a sissy bar" famous for Bloody Mary's, margaritas, daiquiris, and Monday through Thursday happy hour.  Call for our winter hours between January to March.

Experience our famous Flowage Friday Fish Fry starting at noon, or our Wednesday evening Native American Expo, "mid June - mid August" with dancers, singers, and storytellers in full regalia.

The cabin homes are fully equipped and flat on the water waiting for you to enjoy the eagles fly, deer graze, loons sing, bears swim, foxes prance, woodpeckers pecking, and wildlife going about their business as you watch from the deck.
